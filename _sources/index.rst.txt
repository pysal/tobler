Tobler
```````````````````````````````````````````````````````````

:code:`tobler` is a library for areal interpolation and dasymetric mapping. The name is an homage to the legendary geographer `Waldo Tobler`_. It is a subpackage of `PySAL`_ (Python Spatial Analysis Library), and is under active `development`_.
